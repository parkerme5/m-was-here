What defines a collage?

A collage is a work of art created through the act of combining a variety of objects, images, or materials. It involves the purposful shifting of several elements into something entirely new or unexpected. For me, collage is mainly about the collection of older images and how I can transform the past into a present form.

There are many types of collages, but there are a few I focus on:

  • Digital Collage
  • Paper/Magazine Collage
  • Collage Film
